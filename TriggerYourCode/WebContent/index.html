Rules

  1. The game tests your knowledge of various technical topics

  2. You cannot go back at any point and blank answer is considered wrong.

  3. Player need to hit at the centre of the dart board for a level to begin

  4. First is easy level : for each correct answer is +10 and wrong answer gives you -3

  5. There is internal cut off for each level which you have to clear in order to go for the next level

  6. Second level is medium with +20 for each right answer and -5 for each wrong

  7. Third and final is hard level where each right answer is +50 and wrong is -10

  8. Player with the highest score is the winner

  9. In case of any discrepancies the decision of the organizing committee is held final
