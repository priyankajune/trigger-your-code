Rules!


  1. Player need to hit at the centre of the dart board for a level to begin
  2. First is easy level : for each correct answer is +10 and wrong answer gives you -3
  3. There is internal cut off for each level which you have to clear in order to go for the next level
  4. Second level is medium with +20 for each right answer and -5 for each wrong
  5. Third and final is hard level where each right answer is +50 and wrong is -10
  6. Player with the highest score is the winner
  7. In case of any discrepancies the decision of the organizing committee is held final
