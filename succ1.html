Rules!




  1. You will be given 2 hours to solve a maximum of 6 questions.
  2. Participants are not allowed to hit the same number more than twice on the dartboard.
  3. The winner of the game will be decided based on time and score.
  4. Any team found cheating, at any point during the game will be disqualified immediately.
  5. Judges' decision regarding the winners will be final and abiding.
